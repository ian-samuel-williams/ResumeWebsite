Welcome to my website!

My name is Ian Williams and I am currently a Computer Science and Data Science student at Eastern University. I've always enjoyed messing around with computers,
and figuring out what I can do with the resources that I have. I grew up as a missionary child in a small town called Oudorp in the Netherlands, and at 15 moved over
to the US to continue my high school education.

I've always been interested in messing around with electronics. When I was a young kid I was so interested in video games
that I let very little get in my way. Primarily playing on PC, I learned how to use a computer to play video games
and soon figured out that this also meant I could use computers for other things. I learned how to troubleshoot
and the rest is history.
